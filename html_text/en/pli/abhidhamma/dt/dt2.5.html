All the aggregates, all the bases and elements, three truths,
All the faculties, twenty-three dependent originations,
Next sixteen, forty-three triplets,
Seventy-two clusters, seven lesser intermediate couplets,
Eighteen belonging to greater intermediate couplets, and then
Eighteen kinds of last couplets. These (257) kinds of states are expounded,
By the Buddha in the system of Chapter 2.5. The remaining (114) are not expounded.
