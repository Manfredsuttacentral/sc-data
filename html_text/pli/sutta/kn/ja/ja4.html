4. Cūḷaseṭṭhijātaka

“Appakenapi medhāvī,
pābhatena vicakkhaṇo;
Samuṭṭhāpeti attānaṃ,
aṇuṃ aggiṃva sandhaman”ti.

Cūḷaseṭṭhijātakaṃ catutthaṃ.
