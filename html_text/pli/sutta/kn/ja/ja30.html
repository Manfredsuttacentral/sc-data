30. Munikajātaka

“Mā munikassa pihayi,
āturannāni bhuñjati;
Appossukko bhusaṃ khāda,
etaṃ dīghāyulakkhaṇan”ti.

Munikajātakaṃ dasamaṃ.
Kuruṅgavaggo tatiyo.

Tassuddānaṃ

Kuruṅgassa kukkuragojavaro,
Puna vāḷavassasirivhayano;
Mahiḷāmukhanāmanuññavaro,
Vahate dhura munikena dasāti.
