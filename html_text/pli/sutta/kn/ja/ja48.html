48. Vedabbajātaka

“Anupāyena yo atthaṃ,
Icchati so vihaññati;
Cetā haniṃsu vedabbaṃ,
Sabbe te byasanamajjhagūti.

Vedabbajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
