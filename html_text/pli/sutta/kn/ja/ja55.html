55. Pañcāvudhajātaka

“Yo alīnena cittena,
alīnamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.

Pañcāvudhajātakaṃ pañcamaṃ.
