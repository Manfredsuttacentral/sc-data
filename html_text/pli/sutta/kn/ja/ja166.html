166. Upasāḷakajātaka

“Upasāḷakanāmāni,
sahassāni catuddasa;
Asmiṃ padese daḍḍhāni,
natthi loke anāmataṃ.

Yamhi saccañca dhammo ca,
ahiṃsā saṃyamo damo;
Etaṃ ariyā sevanti,
etaṃ loke anāmatan”ti.

Upasāḷakajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
