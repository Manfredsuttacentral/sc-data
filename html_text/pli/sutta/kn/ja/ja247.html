247. Pādañjalījātaka

“Addhā pādañjalī sabbe,
paññāya atirocati;
Tathā hi oṭṭhaṃ bhañjati,
uttariṃ nūna passati”.

“Nāyaṃ dhammaṃ adhammaṃ vā,
Atthānatthañca bujjhati;
Aññatra oṭṭhanibbhogā,
Nāyaṃ jānāti kiñcanan”ti.

Pādañjalījātakaṃ sattamaṃ.
