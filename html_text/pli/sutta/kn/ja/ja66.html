66. Mudulakkhaṇajātaka

“Ekā icchā pure āsi,
aladdhā mudulakkhaṇaṃ;
Yato laddhā aḷārakkhī,
icchā icchaṃ vijāyathā”ti.

Mudulakkhaṇajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
