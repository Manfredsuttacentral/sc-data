73. Saccaṃkirajātaka

“Saccaṃ kirevamāhaṃsu,
narā ekacciyā idha;
Kaṭṭhaṃ niplavitaṃ seyyo,
na tvevekacciyo naro”ti.

Saccaṃkirajātakaṃ tatiyaṃ.
