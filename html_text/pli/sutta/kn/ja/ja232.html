232. Vīṇāguṇajātaka

“Ekacintito yamattho,
bālo apariṇāyako;
Na hi khujjena vāmena,
bhoti saṅgantumarahasi”.

“Purisūsabhaṃ maññamānā,
ahaṃ khujjamakāmayiṃ;
Soyaṃ saṅkuṭito seti,
chinnatanti yathā viṇā”ti.

Vīṇāguṇajātakaṃ dutiyaṃ.
