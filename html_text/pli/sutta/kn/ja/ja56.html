56. Kañcanakkhandhajātaka

“Yo pahaṭṭhena cittena,
pahaṭṭhamanaso naro;
Bhāveti kusalaṃ dhammaṃ,
yogakkhemassa pattiyā;
Pāpuṇe anupubbena,
sabbasaṃyojanakkhayan”ti.

Kañcanakkhandhajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
