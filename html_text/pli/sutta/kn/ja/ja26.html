26. Mahiḷāmukhajātaka

“Purāṇacorāna vaco nisamma,
Mahiḷāmukho pothayamanvacārī;
Susaññatānañhi vaco nisamma,
Gajuttamo sabbaguṇesu aṭṭhā”ti.

Mahiḷāmukhajātakaṃ chaṭṭhaṃ.
