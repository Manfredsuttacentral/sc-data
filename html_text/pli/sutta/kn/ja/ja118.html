118. Vaṭṭakajātaka

“Nācintayanto puriso,
visesamadhigacchati;
Cintitassa phalaṃ passa,
muttosmi vadhabandhanā”ti.

Vaṭṭakajātakaṃ aṭṭhamaṃ.
