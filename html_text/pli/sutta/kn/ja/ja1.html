1. Apaṇṇakajātaka

Namo tassa Bhagavato Arahato Sammāsambuddhassa.

“Apaṇṇakaṃ ṭhānameke,
Dutiyaṃ āhu takkikā;
Etadaññāya medhāvī,
Taṃ gaṇhe yadapaṇṇakan”ti.

Apaṇṇakajātakaṃ paṭhamaṃ.
