19.11. Sekkhattika

Nasekkhaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca asekkho dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā aṭṭhārasa, ārammaṇe nava … pe … āsevane cha … pe … avigate aṭṭhārasa.
