13.21. Ajjhattārammaṇattika

Ajjhattārammaṇaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca naajjhattārammaṇo dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā.

Hetuyā cha, ārammaṇe dve … pe … avigate cha.
