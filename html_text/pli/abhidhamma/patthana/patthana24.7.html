24.7. Hetuduka, Upādānagocchaka

Nahetuṃ naupādānaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca hetu upādāno dhammo uppajjati hetupaccayā. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … avigate nava. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)
