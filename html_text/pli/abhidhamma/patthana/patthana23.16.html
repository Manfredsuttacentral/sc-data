23.16. Kusalattika, Uppannattika

Nakusalo nauppanno dhammo kusalassa uppannassa dhammassa ārammaṇapaccayena paccayo. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Ārammaṇe aṭṭhārasa, adhipatiyā nava, upanissaye aṭṭhārasa.
