9.13. Upādānagocchaka, Kusalattika

Noupādānaṃ nakusalaṃ dhammaṃ paṭicca …. (Saṃkhittaṃ.)

Hetuyā nava … pe … vipāke ekaṃ … pe … avigate nava.
