7. Virtuous conduct

|| lus kyi nes par spyod spaṅs śiṅ ||
lus kyi legs spyad spyad pa’i phyir ||
lus kyi ñes chen las bsruṅs na ||
lus ni bsdam pa byas par ’gyur ||

ṅag gi ñes par spyad spaṅs śiṅ ||
ṅag gi legs spyad spyad pa’i phyir ||
ṅag gi ñes chen las bsruṅs ||
ṅag ni bsdam pa byas par ’gyur ||

yid kyi ñes par spyod spaṅs śiṅ ||
yid kyi legs spyad spyad pa’i phyir ||
yid kyi ñes chen las bsruṅs na ||
yid ni bsdam pa byas par ’gyur ||

lus kyi ñes par spyod spaṅ śiṅ ||
ṅag gi ñes par spyod pa spaṅ ||
yid kyi ñes par spyod spaṅ źiṅ ||
skyon gźan ldan pa gaṅ yin spaṅ ||

lus kyi dge ba rnams bya źiṅ ||
ṅag gi dge ba phal chen bya ||
yid kyi dge ba rnams bya źiṅ ||
tshad med bźi daṅ ldan par bya ||

lus kyi dge ba rnams bya źiṅ ||
ṅag daṅ yid kyis byas na ni ||
’di daṅ pha rol dag tu yaṅ ||
rtag tu bde ba thob par ’gyur ||

rtag tu lus ni bsdams pa’i phyir ||
thub pa ’tshe ba med pa yin ||
de ni ’chi med gnas ’gro źiṅ ||
gaṅ du soṅ na’aṅ mya ṅan med ||

rtag tu ṅag ni bsdams pa’i phyir ||
thub pa ’tshe ba med pa yin ||
de ni ’chi med gnas ’gro źiṅ ||
gaṅ du soṅ na’aṅ mya ṅan med ||

rtag tu yid ni bsdams pa’i phyir ||
thub pa ’tshe ba med pa yin ||
de ni ’chi med gnas ’gro źiṅ ||
gaṅ du soṅ na’aṅ mya ṅan med ||

brtan pa rnams kyis lus bsdam źiṅ ||
brtan pa rnams kyis ṅag kyaṅ bsdam ||
brtan pa rnams kyis yid bsdam źiṅ ||
brtan pas kun du bsdams byas na ||
de dag ’chi med gnas ’gro źiṅ ||
gaṅ du soṅ yaṅ mya ṅan med ||

lus kyi sdom pa legs pa ste ||
ṅag gi sdom pa legs pa yin ||
yid kyi sdom pa’aṅ legs pa ste ||
thams cad du ni bsdams pa legs ||
kun du bsdams pa’i dge sloṅ ni ||
sdug bsṅal kun las rnam par ’grol ||

ṅag rnams bsruṅ źiṅ yid ni legs bsdams te ||
lus kyis mi dge ba dag spyad mi bya ||
las lam gsum po ’di dag rnam sbyaṅs na ||
draṅ sroṅ gsuṅs pa’i lam ni thob par ’gyur ||

legs par spyad pa’i tshoms te bdun pa’o ||

Translation by Vidyākaraprabhā and Rin chen mchog.

9–10th century A.D.
