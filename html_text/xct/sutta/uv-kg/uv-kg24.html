24. Numbers (or comparisons)

|| gaṅ dag don daṅ mi ldan tshig ||
tshigs bcad brgya dag smra ba bas ||
gaṅ dag ñe bar źi ’gyur ba’i ||
don ldan tshig gcig thos pa ruṅ ||

gaṅ dag chos daṅ mi ldan tshig ||
tshigs bcad brgya dag smra ba bas ||
gaṅ dag ne bar źi ’gyur ba’i ||
chos ldan tsig gcig thos pa ruṅ ||

tshul ’chal mñam par ma bźag pa ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
tshul khrims ldan źiṅ mñam bźag pa ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

brtson med le lo ldan pa dag ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
brtson ’grus brtan pa brtsams pa dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

śes ’chal mñam par ma bźag pa ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
śes rab chen po mñam bźag pa ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

skye daṅ ’jig pa ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
skye daṅ ’jig pa mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

tshor ba zad pa ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
tshor bzad pa mthoṅ pa dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

zag pa zad pa ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
zag pa zad pa mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

go ’phaṅ mi g-yo ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas||
go ’phaṅ mi g-yo mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

go ’phaṅ blta dka’ ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
go ’phaṅ blta dka’ mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

go ’phaṅ mthon po ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
go ’phaṅ mthon po mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

go ’phaṅ dam pa ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
go ’phaṅ dam pa mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

’chi med go ’phaṅ ma mthoṅ ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
’chi med go ’phaṅ mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

bdud rtsi’i go ’phaṅ ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
bdud rtsi’i go ’phaṅ mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

rdul me go ’phaṅ ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
rdul med go ’phaṅ mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

rdul bral go ’phaṅ ma mthoṅ ba ||
lo brgyar gson pa gaṅ yin pas ||
rdul bral go ’phaṅ mthoṅ ba dag ||
ñin źag ’ga’ źig gson pa ruṅ ||

mi ni lo brgyar nags tshal na ||
me la yoṅs sten gaṅ yin daṅ ||
bdag ñid bsgom la yud tsam źig ||
mchod pa byed pa gaṅ yin pa ||
mchod byed de ñid ches ruṅ gi ||
lo brgyar sbyin sreg byed mi ruṅ ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅayina des ||
saṅs rgyas la ni dad pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des||
dam pa’i chos la dad pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des ||
dge ’dun la ni dad pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des ||
sems can rnams la sñiṅ brtse ba’i ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des ||
srog chags rnams la sñiṅ brtse ba’i ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des ||
’byuṅ po rnams la sñiṅ brtse ba’i ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des ||
byams pas sems par byed pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer ku śa’i rtses ||
kha zas za ba gaṅ yin des||
legs par gsuṅs pa’i dam chos kyi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer mchod sbyin stoṅ ||
lo brgyar byed pa gaṅ yin des ||
saṅs rgyas la ni dad pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer mchog sbyin stoṅ ||
lo brgyar byed pa gaṅ yin des ||
dam pa’i chos la dad pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer mchod sbyin stoṅ ||
lo brgyar byed pa gaṅ yin des ||

bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

’byuṅ po rnams la sñiṅ brtse ba’i ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

lo brgyar byed pa gaṅ yin des ||
byams par sems par byed pa yi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

zla ba re rer mchod sbyin stoṅ ||
lo brgyar byed pa gaṅ yin des ||
legs par gsuṅs pa’i dam chos kyi ||
bcu drug char yaṅ mi phod do ||

bsod nams ’dod pa’i mi yis ’jig rten du ||
sbyin sreg mchod sbyin chuṅ zad ci byas des ||
gaṅ źig draṅ po’i sems kyis phyag ’tshal ba ||
de yi bźi char yaṅ ni mi phod do ||

bsgre ba’i tshoms te ñi śu rtsa bźi pa’o ||
