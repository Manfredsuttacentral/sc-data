22. Śrutavarga

[Stances 1–4 perdues]

pradīpena tu rūpāṇi
. . paṣyati cakṣumān |
evaṃ śrutvā vijānāti
dharmmaṃ kalyāṇa-pāpakaṃ

bahu-śruto ’pi ca bhavati
śīleṣu asamāhitaḥ
. . . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . .

alpa-śruto ’pi ca bhavati
śīleṣu susamāhitaḥ |
śīlatainaṃ praśaṃsanti
tasya saṃpadyate śrutaṃ

alpa-śrulo ’pi ca bhavati
śīleṣu asamāhitaḥ
. . . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . .

bahu-śruto ’pi ca bhavati
śīlesu susamāhitaḥ |
ubhayatainaṃ praśaṃsanti
tasya saṃpadyate śrutaṃ

bahu-śruto dharmma-dharo
prajñavāṃ pratibhānavān |
. . . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . .

ye mā rūpeṇa pramiṇīnsu
ye mā ghoṣeṇa anvagaḥ |
cchanda-rāga-vaśopetā
na me jānanti te janā

adhyātmañ ca na jānāti
bahirddhā ca na paśyati |
samaṃtāvaraṇo bālaḥ
. . . .    .nīyate

adhyātmaṇ ca na jānāti
bahirddhā ca vipaśyati |
addhyatmaṃ phala-darśāvī
so ’pi ghoṣeṇa nīyate

addhyamañ ca vijānāti
bahirddhā .   . . . .
. . . .    . . . .
so ’pi ghoṣeṇa nīyate

adhyātmañ ca prajānāti
bahirddhā ca vipaśyati |
. . niḥsaraṇa-prājño
nāpi ghoṣeṇa. . .

. . . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . .
na ca dṛṣṭaṃ śrutaṃ
dhīraḥ sarvvaṃ śraddhātum arhati

vijñāna-sārāṇi subhāṣitāni
. . . . . . samādhi-sāram |
na tasya . . . . . . . .
. . . . . . . . . . .

dharmme tu ye āryya-pravedite ratā
tathā caranti vacasā karmmaṇā ca ; |
te ṣīla-sauratya-samādhi-gaucarāḥ
śrutasya prajñāya ca sāram adhyagāt
