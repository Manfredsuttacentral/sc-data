• Udānavarga de Subaši

19. Aśvavarga

aśvo yathā bhadra kaśābhispṛṣṭaḥ
ātapinaḥ . . . . . . .
śraddhāya śīelna ca vīryyavāṃs ta-
thā
samādhinā dharmma-viniścayena |
sampanna-vidyā-caraṇām pratismṛtām
prahāsyate sarvva-bhavāni tādṛśāḥ

. . . . . . . . . . .
ātāpinaḥ saṃvijitaś carantaḥ |
śraddhāya sīlena ca vīryyavaṃs tathā
samādhinā dharmma-viniścayena ca |
te kṣānti-sauratya-samāhitendriyāḥ
śrutasya . . . . . . . . .

. . . . . samatāṇ gatāni
aśvo yathā sārathinā sudāntaḥ |
prahīṇa-krodhasya-m-anāsravasya
devāpi tasya smṛhayanti tādṛṇaḥ

. . . .    . . . . |
appramattaḥ pramatteṣu
abalāśvan va bhadraśva
hitvā yānti sumedhasaḥ

hirī-niṣevī puruṣaḥ
prajñavāṃ susamāhitaḥ |
sarvva-pāpan apohati
bhadraḥ aśva kaśām iva

dānto . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . . |
dantaḥ śreṣṭo manuṣyāṇāṃ
yo ’tivākyaṃ titīkṣati

yo aśvataraṃ damayed
ajāneyan va saindhavaṃ |
kuñjaraṃ vā mahā-nāgaṃ
atma-dantas tato vara

na hi . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . . . |
yathātmanā sudāntena
dānto dāntena gacchati

atmānam eva damayed
āśva bhadram va sārathiḥ |
atmā hi ātmanaṃ dāntạh
smṛtimaṃ duḥkha-pāragaḥ

. . . .    . . . .
. . . .    . . gatiḥ |
tasmāt sanyamayātmānaṃ
aśva bhadraṃ va vānija

aśva-varga ekūna-vīśati | | * | |

Nakatani, Hideaki
