source: Ernst Waldschmidt: Bruchstücke des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa der Sarvāstivādins, mit einer Darstellung der Überlieferung des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa in den verschiedenen Schulen, Leipzig, 1926. pp. 21–40

Manuscript found during the German Turfan expeditions.

Waldschmidt ID: Pa; alternative ID: Kat.-Nr. 44, refering to the "Sanskrithandschriften aus den Turfanfunden" (SHT) catalogue of the German Turfan expeditions. Now available at the international dunhuang project website, idp.bl.uk.

find spot: Qyzil

explanation of abbreviations: Manuscript IDs: manuscript consists of 14 pieces, numbered Pa1-Pa14 - each with text on recto and verso, marked with r and v, thus p. ex. Pa1r.

⊙ : string hole
