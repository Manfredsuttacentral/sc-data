source: Ernst Waldschmidt: Bruchstücke des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa der Sarvāstivādins, mit einer Darstellung der Überlieferung des Bhikṣuṇī-Prātimokṣa in den verschiedenen Schulen, Leipzig, 1926. pp. 42–46

Manuscript found during the German Turfan expeditions.

Waldschmidt ID: V; alternative ID: Kat.-Nr. 394, refering to the "Sanskrithandschriften aus den Turfanfunden" (SHT) catalogue of the German Turfan expeditions. Now available at the international dunhuang project website, idp.bl.uk. The database includes a (copyrighted) photo of V = 394

find spot: Murtuq (according to Waldschmidt) / Šorčuq (according to SHT catalogue)

explanation of abbreviations: Manuscript IDs: manuscript consists of 3 pieces, Va, Vb, Vc - each with text on recto and verso, marked with r and v, thus p. ex. Var.

⊙ : string hole
